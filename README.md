# 🏟️ SmartStadium AI — FIFA World Cup 2026

> GenAI-powered intelligent platform for FIFA World Cup 2026
> stadium operations — serving Fans, Organizers, Volunteers
> and Venue Staff simultaneously.

## 🎯 Problem
50,000+ fans in one stadium struggle with navigation,
food finding, schedule queries, and emergency situations.
Staff get overwhelmed with repetitive questions.

## 💡 Solution
SmartStadium AI — a GenAI chatbot powered by Claude AI
that acts as a personal stadium assistant for every fan,
available in all languages, answering any question in seconds.

## ✨ Features
- 🗺️ Real-time seat & navigation assistance
- 🍕 Food court locations & menu info
- ⚽ Match schedules & player statistics
- 🚨 Emergency guidance & safety info
- 🚗 Parking & transport directions
- 🌍 Multilingual support (auto-detect)
- ♿ Accessibility assistance
- 🎛️ Staff operational intelligence
- 👥 Crowd management support
